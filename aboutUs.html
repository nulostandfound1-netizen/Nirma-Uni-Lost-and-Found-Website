About US

A simple and reliable lost-and-found solution for our campus community.

1.


Who We Are

We are a campus-based Lost & Found platform created to support students, faculty, and staff by providing a centralized and trustworthy system for reporting lost and found items.

2.


What Do We Do

We help users report lost belongings, register found items, and search through existing entries in order to connect items with their rightful owners swiftly and smoothly.

3.


How Do We Help

We then use a simple, secure, and user-friendly digital platform where users can submit the details of items, view the updates, and communicate responsibly-no manual notices and no confusion.

4.


Our Mission

Our mission is to create a responsible campus community by bringing transparency, efficiency, and reliability to the lost-and-found process using technology.
